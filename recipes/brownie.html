Solid Brownie recipe!

By yours truly

A solid and strong brownie straight from the oven just waiting for you!

Some say that sweets are the foundation for a great relationship...

So, how about making some strong and solid brownies for your partner?

Ingredient list:

  • 300g of flour cement powder

  • 200g of chocolate gravel

  • 30 drops of chocolate stones

  • 150g of sugar crunchy sand

  • 10 watery eggs

How to prepare:

  1. Mix the powder ingredients first. If needed, use your hands

  2. Now add the watery eggs, the gravel and stones

  3. Be careful now, since it will be a bit solid. Add the mix to a sheet and yeet it in the oven

  4. Bake it slowly at 180 Â°C for over 1 hour and half

  5. After baking it, it's ready to serve!

You can use it in multiple ways, from sustaining your table or just eating with strong teeth!

Who knows? Maybe you can open your solid bakery one day with this recipe!
